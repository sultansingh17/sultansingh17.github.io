About Our School

Montessori Bal Niketan Uchch Mahavidyalaya (MBN SCHOOL) is committed to providing quality education with discipline, values and modern learning facilities. Our mission is to develop confident, responsible and successful students through academic excellence, cultural activities, sports, NCC, environmental awareness and personality development.

Founder & Director

Shri Sultan Singh Tanwar

Founder & Director of Montessori Bal Niketan Uchch Mahavidyalaya (MBN SCHOOL). His dedication towards education, discipline and social service has helped thousands of students achieve success.

Activities & Achievements

🌱 Plantation Drive

MBN School regularly conducts plantation drives to encourage environmental awareness and responsibility among students.

🏆 Awards & Recognition

Students are honoured for excellence in academics, sports, culture and leadership throughout the year.

🎭 Cultural Events

Our Legacy

Established in 1974, Montessori Bal Niketan Uchch Mahavidyalaya (MBN SCHOOL) has served generations of students in Jaisalmer with dedication and commitment to quality education. Under the guidance of Shri Sultan Singh Tanwar, the institution continues to inspire students to achieve academic excellence while developing strong moral values and leadership skills.
